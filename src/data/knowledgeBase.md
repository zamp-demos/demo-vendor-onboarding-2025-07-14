# Conscient Infrastructure - Vendor Onboarding & Compliance SOP

## 1. Overview

This knowledge base governs the vendor onboarding process for Conscient Infrastructure Private Limited. All construction material suppliers, contractors, and service providers must complete this process before any purchase orders are issued.

**Scope**: Applies to all new vendors across residential projects (Elaira, Heritage Max, PARQ) in Gurgaon, Goa, and Dehradun.

## 2. Vendor Categories

| Category | Examples | Risk Tier |
|----------|----------|-----------|
| Cement & Concrete | UltraTech, ACC, Ambuja | Standard |
| Steel & TMT Bars | Jindal, Tata Steel, SAIL | Standard |
| HVAC & MEP | Daikin, Carrier, Voltas | High (safety-critical) |

## 3. Required Documents

### All Vendors
- GSTIN Certificate (must be Active status)
- PAN Card
- Company Registration / CIN
- Bank Account Details with cancelled cheque
- Last 2 years audited financial statements
- Certificate of Insurance (COI) with minimum coverage

### Category-Specific
- **Safety-critical (HVAC, Electrical)**: Valid contractor license, safety certifications (OHSAS/ISO 45001)
- **Cement/Steel**: BIS certification for products
- **Interior/Fit-out**: Portfolio of past 3 projects, client references

## 4. Verification Steps

### Step 1: GST Verification
- Cross-check GSTIN on gst.gov.in portal
- Status must be **Active** (not Suspended, Cancelled, or Inactive)
- Registered address must match submitted application
- Last return filing date must be within 2 months

### Step 2: PAN & Company Verification
- Validate PAN against Income Tax database
- Verify CIN on MCA portal
- Check for any pending litigation or NCLT cases

### Step 3: Financial Health Assessment
- Review audited P&L and Balance Sheet
- Check credit rating (CRISIL/ICRA if available)
- Minimum annual turnover thresholds:
  - Standard vendors: Rs. 5 Crore
  - High-value contracts (>Rs. 10 Cr): Rs. 25 Crore
  - Safety-critical: Rs. 10 Crore

### Step 4: RERA Compliance Check
- Search vendor on State RERA portals for any penalties or complaints
- Check if vendor was involved in any project delays exceeding 6 months
- Verify no active complaints against the vendor

### Step 5: Insurance Verification
- COI must be current (not expired)
- Minimum coverage requirements:
  - Standard: Rs. 2 Crore
  - High-value contracts: Rs. 5 Crore
  - Safety-critical: Rs. 10 Crore
- Coverage must include third-party liability

### Step 6: Reference Check
- Contact minimum 2 of 3 submitted references
- Rating scale: Excellent / Good / Satisfactory / Poor
- All references must be Satisfactory or above

## 5. Risk Scoring

| Score Range | Rating | Action |
|-------------|--------|--------|
| 80-100 | LOW | Auto-approve, create vendor master |
| 60-79 | MEDIUM | Procurement manager review required |
| 40-59 | MEDIUM-HIGH | Senior procurement review + additional due diligence |
| 0-39 | HIGH | Reject or escalate to VP Procurement |

### Scoring Weights
- GST Status: 20 points
- Financial Health: 25 points
- RERA Compliance: 20 points
- Insurance Status: 15 points
- References: 10 points
- BIS/ISO Certifications: 10 points

## 6. HITL Decision Points

### Mandatory Human Approval Required When:
- GSTIN status is not Active
- Address mismatch between application and government records
- RERA penalty found in last 3 years
- Insurance expired or coverage below minimum
- Risk score below 60

### Auto-Approval Allowed When:
- All documents valid and verified
- Risk score 80 or above
- No RERA penalties in history
- Active insurance with adequate coverage

## 7. Post-Onboarding

- Vendor added to ERP with unique vendor code
- Welcome email with procurement portal credentials
- 90-day probationary review for conditional approvals
- Annual re-verification for all active vendors
- Insurance renewal tracking with 30-day advance alerts

## 8. Escalation Matrix

| Issue | Escalate To | SLA |

| RERA penalty | Senior Procurement | 3 business days |
